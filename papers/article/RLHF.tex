\section{Reinforcement Learning with Human Feedback\label{sec:rlhf}}

Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF) is closely related to IRL and has attracted recently
for its use in helping to ``align'' large language models (LLM). In RLHF an initially
trained AI model such as a LLM learns to further optimize its performance based on direct human feedback.
Instead of relying solely on pre-defined reward functions, RLHF uses IRL methods to learn human preferencs
over the behavior and output of the AI model, and once those preferences are learned, the AI model/LLM
can be further trained to produce output that is more aligned with human preferences and goals.
