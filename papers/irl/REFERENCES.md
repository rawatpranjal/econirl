# IRL Literature References

Papers referenced in econirl IRL dataset implementations.

## Ziebart et al. (2008) - Maximum Entropy IRL

**Title:** Maximum Entropy Inverse Reinforcement Learning

**Authors:** Ziebart, B. D., Maas, A., Bagnell, J. A., & Dey, A. K.

**Venue:** AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence

**Year:** 2008

**URL:** https://www.cs.cmu.edu/~bziebart/publications/maximum-entropy-inverse-reinforcement-learning.pdf

**Notes:** Foundational MaxEnt IRL paper - freely available

---

## Abbeel & Ng (2004) - Apprenticeship Learning

**Title:** Apprenticeship Learning via Inverse Reinforcement Learning

**Authors:** Abbeel, P., & Ng, A. Y.

**Venue:** ICML

**Year:** 2004

**Notes:** Foundational IRL paper

---

## Yuan et al. (2010) - T-Drive

**Title:** T-Drive: Driving Directions Based on Taxi Trajectories

**Authors:** Yuan, J., Zheng, Y., Zhang, C., Xie, W., Xie, X., Sun, G., & Huang, Y.

**Venue:** ACM SIGSPATIAL GIS

**Year:** 2010

**URL:** https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/publication/t-drive-trajectory-data-sample/

**Notes:** T-Drive taxi trajectory dataset

---

## Zheng et al. (2008-2010) - GeoLife

**Title:** GeoLife GPS Trajectory Dataset

**Authors:** Zheng, Y., et al.

**Institution:** Microsoft Research

**URL:** https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/download/details.aspx?id=52367

**Notes:** Human mobility GPS trajectories with transportation mode labels

---

## Robicquet et al. (2016) - Stanford Drone Dataset

**Title:** Learning Social Etiquette: Human Trajectory Understanding in Crowded Scenes

**Authors:** Robicquet, A., Sadeghian, A., Alahi, A., & Savarese, S.

**Venue:** ECCV

**Year:** 2016

**URL:** https://cvgl.stanford.edu/projects/uav_data/

**Notes:** Stanford Drone Dataset paper

---

## Pellegrini et al. (2009) - ETH Pedestrian Dataset

**Title:** You'll Never Walk Alone: Modeling Social Behavior for Multi-target Tracking

**Authors:** Pellegrini, S., Ess, A., Schindler, K., & Van Gool, L.

**Venue:** ICCV

**Year:** 2009

**Notes:** ETH pedestrian dataset paper

---

## Lerner et al. (2007) - UCY Dataset

**Title:** Crowds by Example

**Authors:** Lerner, A., Chrysanthou, Y., & Lischinski, D.

**Venue:** Computer Graphics Forum

**Year:** 2007

**Notes:** UCY pedestrian dataset paper
