# DDC Literature References

Papers referenced in econirl DDC dataset implementations.

## Rust (1987) - Bus Engine Replacement

**Title:** Optimal Replacement of GMC Bus Engines: An Empirical Model of Harold Zurcher

**Authors:** Rust, J.

**Journal:** Econometrica, 55(5), 999-1033

**DOI:** [10.2307/1911259](https://doi.org/10.2307/1911259)

**Notes:** Foundational NFXP paper. NFXP manual available at: https://editorialexpress.com/jrust/nfxp.pdf

---

## Keane & Wolpin (1994) - Simulation and Interpolation

**Title:** The Solution and Estimation of Discrete Choice Dynamic Programming Models by Simulation and Interpolation: Monte Carlo Evidence

**Authors:** Keane, M. P., & Wolpin, K. I.

**Journal:** Review of Economics and Statistics, 76(4), 648-672

**DOI:** [10.2307/2109768](https://doi.org/10.2307/2109768)

**Notes:** Available via JSTOR with institutional access

---

## Keane & Wolpin (1997) - Career Decisions

**Title:** The Career Decisions of Young Men

**Authors:** Keane, M. P., & Wolpin, K. I.

**Journal:** Journal of Political Economy, 105(3), 473-522

**DOI:** [10.1086/262080](https://doi.org/10.1086/262080)

**Notes:** Available via JPE with institutional access

---

## Hotz & Miller (1993) - CCP Foundation

**Title:** Conditional Choice Probabilities and the Estimation of Dynamic Models

**Authors:** Hotz, V. J., & Miller, R. A.

**Journal:** Review of Economic Studies, 60(3), 497-529

**DOI:** [10.2307/2297853](https://doi.org/10.2307/2297853)

**Notes:** Foundational CCP paper

---

## Aguirregabiria & Mira (2010) - Survey

**Title:** Dynamic Discrete Choice Structural Models: A Survey

**Authors:** Aguirregabiria, V., & Mira, P.

**Journal:** Journal of Econometrics, 156(1), 38-67

**URL:** http://aguirregabiria.net/wpapers/survey_annalsje.pdf

**Notes:** Comprehensive survey of DDC methods
